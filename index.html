Why staking with GranADA?

0% fees

We want to offer the fairest staking conditions to our delegators. Specially for those who come from Latin America.

Education

Our goal is to educate and inform, with more focus on the Spanish-Speaking community, about blockchain technologies and the Cardano ecosystem.

Decentralization

We are a truly decentralized staking pool with a hybrid node implementation (data center and private server).

Availability

Our servers are 24/7 online, so we never miss a block.

Social commitment

We monthly contribute to the WFP to help families in need living in countries with humanitarian crisis. Delegate kindness with us ❤️

IT know-how

Our servers are updated, maintained and constantly monitored by a software engineer with experience in DevOps, .NET technologies and full-stack development.

Security

Best cybersecurity practices were implemented on our servers: SSH, 2FA, firewalls, port blocking, IP banning, etc.

Sustainability

GranADA Staking Pool is powered with renewable energy.

100% cruelty-free

Everyone is vegan in our gran team. Therefore, our operations are 100% free from animal exploitation.
